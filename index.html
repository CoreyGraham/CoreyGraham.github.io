About Me

Hi! My name is Corey and I am a game Design and Programming student from the University of Staffordshire. At the age of 11, I decided I wanted to make games and began my journey with the web engine Scratch, making games based of my favorite franchises. In the last few years I have branched out to Unity and Unreal Engine through my studies and am eager to push my skills to persue a career in this industry.

Here you can see the projects I've had the opportunity to be apart of and my own personal projects I've been working on. I have a strong determination towards the work I do and am always eager to tackle whatever challenges I'm presented. I often take my work above and beyond, to fully realise the creative vision and ensure it's executed in the best possible way.

I also enjoy programming that's unrelated to games, like this website for instance. Feel free to look under the surface of this website to see how I made this :)
